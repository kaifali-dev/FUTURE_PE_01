# Prompt Analysis

## Why Target Audience Was Included

The target audience was included to help the AI generate content that directly addresses the needs, concerns, and expectations of potential customers. This ensures the website copy is relevant and persuasive.

## Why USP Was Included

The Unique Selling Proposition (USP) helps differentiate the business from competitors. Including the USP ensures that the generated content highlights the unique value offered by RACE.

## Why Website Goals Were Included

Website goals provide direction to the AI and ensure the generated content supports business objectives such as generating inquiries, building trust, educating customers, and encouraging service bookings.

## Prompt Engineering Approach

The prompt was structured using:

* Business Context
* Target Audience
* USP
* Website Objectives
* Required Website Sections
* Writing Requirements

This approach helps produce business-specific, conversion-focused website copy instead of generic content.
